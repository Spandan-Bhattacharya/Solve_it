How to Play Rock-Paper-Scissors:

1. Rock, Paper, Scissors is a hand game usually played between two people, where each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand.
2. The shapes are "rock" (a fist), "paper" (an open hand), and "scissors" (a fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V).
3. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. If both players choose the same shape, the game is a tie and is usually played again.
4. Players typically count to three in unison and then simultaneously reveal their hands.
