How to Play Tic Tac Toe:

1. Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game played on a 3x3 grid.
2. The players take turns marking a square with their symbol, either "X" or "O".
3. The first player to get three of their symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, wins the game.
4. If all squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a draw.
