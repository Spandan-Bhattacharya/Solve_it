How to Play Snake:

1. Snake is a classic arcade game where the player controls a snake that moves around the screen, eating food and growing in length.
2. The snake's length increases each time it eats food, and the player must avoid running into the walls of the playing area or into the snake's own body.
3. The game ends if the snake runs into a wall or into itself.
4. The objective is to eat as much food as possible to score points and grow the snake as long as possible.
