How to Play Hangman:

1. Hangman is a word-guessing game where one player thinks of a word and the other player tries to guess it by suggesting letters within a certain number of guesses.
2. The word to guess is represented by a row of dashes, representing each letter of the word.
3. The player guessing the word selects a letter. If the letter is in the word, it is revealed in the correct positions. If not, a part of a hanged man is drawn as a penalty.
4. The game ends when the word is guessed correctly (all letters are revealed) or when the hanged man is completed (all guesses are used).
