How to Play Bingo:

1. Each player gets a bingo card with a 5x5 grid containing numbers.
2. The game host randomly selects numbers and calls them out.
3. Players mark off the called numbers on their cards.
4. The first player to complete a row, column, or diagonal on their card shouts "Bingo!" and wins the game.
5. In some variations, additional patterns or rules may be used to win.
