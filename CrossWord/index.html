How to Play Crossword:

1. Crossword is a word puzzle game where you fill in the white squares with letters to form words, which then intersect with each other.
2. Each word is clued, either directly or indirectly, and the player must figure out the word based on the clue.
3. Words are placed in the grid from left to right or top to bottom, and each square can be part of two words, across and down.
4. The game is won when all the white squares are filled in correctly, and all the clues are satisfied.
