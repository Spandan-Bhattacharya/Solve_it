How to Play Ping Pong:

1. Ping Pong, also known as table tennis, is a game played on a rectangular table with a net across the middle.
2. Players use paddles to hit a lightweight ball back and forth, with the objective of scoring points by making the ball land on the opponent's side of the table in a way that the opponent cannot return it.
3. A point is scored when the opponent fails to return the ball within the rules. The game is typically played to 11 points, and the player or team that reaches 11 points first wins the game.
