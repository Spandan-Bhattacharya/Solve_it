How to Play 8 Puzzle:

1. Arrange the tiles in order from 1 to 8, leaving the last square empty.
2. Click on a tile adjacent to the empty square to move it into the empty space.
3. Continue moving tiles until they are arranged in numerical order from 1 to 8.
4. You can only move tiles into the empty square, and only one tile at a time.
5. Try to solve the puzzle in the fewest moves possible!
