About SOLVE IT!
Solve it! was born when it was felt that there was a need for a place where people could come after trying to solve a puzzle and get the solution with ease.
We hope that when you come to our site with a problem, you go back satisfied with the solution and a love for some other game you found on our site.
Join us on this journey of exploration and problem-solving. Whether you're a novice or an experienced puzzle enthusiast, we have something for everyone.
