How to Play Connect 4:

1. Connect 4 is played on a vertical board with 7 columns and 6 rows.
2. Players take turns dropping their colored discs from the top into a column.
3. The discs fall straight down, occupying the lowest available space in the column.
4. The objective is to be the first to connect four of your discs in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
5. If the board fills up without a winner, the game is a draw.
