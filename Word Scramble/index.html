How to Play Word Scramble:

1. Word Scramble is a game where players try to unscramble letters to form words.
2. A scrambled word is displayed on the screen, and the player must rearrange the letters to form a valid word.
3. The player can rearrange the letters by clicking on them and dragging them to the correct positions.
4. If the player is stuck, they can use a hint to reveal one letter of the word.
5. The game can be played against a timer, and the player can try to unscramble as many words as possible before time runs out.
